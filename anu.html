ONE EVENT, THOUSAND SMILES

Team Aashraya has conduncted over 350 events in the past two years, in order to raise funds for the underpriviliged. Some of these events include free health camp, teaching camps, sanitation workshops, a rally for protection of children against labour and workshops for education of rural women. We, at Aashraya believe that happiness increases by sharing and this is the motto we live by.
